Origin

The legend of the cat who refused to run.

In the trenches of crypto twitter, every creature was running. Dogs chased the next bone. Frogs leapt from one chart to another, never stopping for even a second. Everyone believed: whoever ran the fastest would be the first to reach the moon.

Then, in the middle of all that chaos, one cat stopped. It didn't join the run. It sat.

Not because it was slow. Not because it was afraid. The cat sat because it knew one thing everyone else had forgotten: a red candle is only scary to those standing and trembling, not to those sitting calmly.

People laughed at it. "Why are you sitting? Everyone else is running!" But every time the market panicked, every time the chart crashed and everyone sold at a loss out of fear, the cat was still in the exact same spot. Going nowhere. Needing to go nowhere.

And when the chart finally turned around — like it always does — only one was still in position: the cat that chose to sit from the very beginning.

That's Sits Cat. Not just a funny cat meme. It's a simple reminder for anyone tired of following the market's panic: sometimes, the way to win is to not run at all.
